My Projects

Photoelectric Effect Simulation

The photoelectric effect simulation demonstrates how light shining on a metal surface ejects electrons, showing that light energy is transferred to electrons as kinetic energy. Users can adjust light frequency and intensity to observe how higher frequencies enable electron emission, while intensity influences the number of emitted electrons without affecting their energy. This simulation visually captures the quantum nature of light.

A smart recycle bin is an automated waste-sorting system equipped with sensors and AI.
